About Discerning Eye Community Agriculture

The measure of who qualifies as an at-risk community seems to be growing at an unsustainable rate. In the midst of this growth, there is an increasing percentage of food insecure households not only in America but around the world, particularly in urban environments. This increase causes irreparable damage to the health and wellness of individuals and the communities in which they reside. Nutrient deficiency is the leading cause of skin, liver, blood and heart diseases, hormonal imbalance, can cause cancer, dementia and other brain diseases as well. These ailments can drain families of their money and longevity. DECA aims to fill the gap and dinner tables with convienient, accessible, fresh food options, educational resources on how to sustain a healthy lifestyle through the act of farming, and provide participants access to the financial benefits of inclusion the fresh food industry.

Discerning Eye Community Agriculture, DECA, is a non-profit organization whose mission is to Is to Create Wellness and Abundance in Our Communities Through The Acts of Gardening and Farming, Creating Communal Agency and Inclusion in the Fresh Food Industry.



Using a hybrid social enterprise model, DECA, gives back to the community through urban revitalization and public/private garden farm projects and hosts three sister companies, BFC, DCP and DCF
